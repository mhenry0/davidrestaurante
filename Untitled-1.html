Sobre Nosotros


Welcome to Potz Bistro – Ojochal’s Premier Dining Destination

Nestled in the picturesque town of Ojochal, Potz Bistro opens its doors in 2024,

bringing a fresh and vibrant dining experience to this charming Costa Rican locale.

At Potz Bistro, we combine a passion for exceptional food with a warm, inviting

atmosphere, creating the perfect setting for any occasion.

Our menu is a celebration of flavors, offering a diverse selection of dishes crafted

from the finest locally-sourced ingredients. From succulent seafood and expertly

grilled meats to innovative vegetarian options, each dish at Potz Bistro is designed

to tantalize your taste buds and showcase the rich culinary heritage of the region.

Step into our stylish yet cozy bistro and enjoy an unforgettable meal in a space

that blends contemporary elegance with rustic charm. Our attentive and friendly

staff are dedicated to providing a memorable dining experience, whether you’re here

for a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or a casual lunch with friends.

Complement your meal with our curated selection of fine wines and artisanal cocktails,

and indulge in decadent desserts that promise to end your dining experience on a sweet note.

Join us at Potz Bistro in 2024 and discover why we’re quickly becoming Ojochal’s

favorite spot for exquisite food and exceptional service. We look forward to

welcoming you!
